What is this series about?





Jujutsu Kaisen starts with an easygoing and kind-hearted highschooler named Itadori Yuuji, who decides to join his school's Occult Club. When he and his friends accidentally summon grotesque creatures by messing with a cursed object, Itadori meets a boy named Fushiguro Megumi and they both fight to save his friends' lives.

While trying to save Fushiguro from a curse, Itadori ends up swallowing a cursed finger and becomes Sukuna's vessel. He then meets a teacher from Jujutsu High named Gojo Satoru, who saves him from his execution.

Itadori ends up studying at Jujutsu High as a first year, along with Fushiguro Megumi and Kugisaki Nobara.

This mysterious, dark, and supernatural anime series was created by Gege Akutami
